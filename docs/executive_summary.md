# Executive Summary  
## Revenue Leakage & Retention Command Center

---

## 1. Problem Statement

Mid-sized B2B organizations often experience **significant hidden revenue loss** despite stable or growing top-line revenue.

This loss typically occurs through:
- Customer churn
- Revenue contraction
- Excessive discounting
- Billing and collection inefficiencies

These issues are **fragmented across systems and teams**, making them difficult to detect and act on in a timely manner.

As a result, leadership lacks a **single, trusted view of revenue health**, leading to delayed decisions and preventable revenue loss.

---

## 2. Objective

The goal of this solution is to:

> Provide a centralized, decision-driven analytics platform that identifies, quantifies, and helps reduce revenue leakage while improving customer retention.

---

## 3. Solution Overview

The **Revenue Leakage & Retention Command Center** is an end-to-end BI solution that integrates data across revenue functions and translates it into **clear, actionable insights**.

It enables:

- Executive visibility into revenue health
- Early detection of at-risk customers
- Identification of revenue leakage drivers
- Performance accountability across teams

The solution is structured into five core analytical views:

1. Executive Overview  
2. Revenue Leakage Analysis  
3. Customer Risk & Retention  
4. Account Manager / Region Performance  
5. Customer Drilldown  

---

## 4. Key Business Impact

This solution directly supports:

### 4.1 Revenue Protection
- Identifies leakage sources such as discounting and churn
- Enables targeted interventions to recover lost revenue

### 4.2 Retention Improvement
- Detects early warning signals of customer risk
- Supports proactive customer success strategies

### 4.3 Operational Efficiency
- Highlights inefficiencies in billing and collections
- Improves cash flow visibility (DSO reduction)

### 4.4 Accountability & Performance
- Enables performance tracking at manager and regional levels
- Aligns teams around shared revenue KPIs

---

## 5. Example Use Cases

- A VP Revenue identifies that **NRR dropped from 104% to 96%**, driven primarily by contraction in a specific region
- Finance detects that **discount rates exceed targets for certain account managers**
- Customer Success flags **high-value accounts with declining usage and increased support tickets**
- Leadership identifies **$X in at-risk revenue** and prioritizes intervention

---

## 6. Design Approach

This solution was developed using a **business-first, KPI-driven methodology**:

- Stakeholder requirements defined before data modeling
- KPIs standardized and documented before implementation
- Star schema data model designed for scalability and clarity
- Dashboards structured around decisions, not just visuals

---

## 7. Technology Stack

- **Database:** PostgreSQL (pgAdmin)
- **Data Modeling:** Star Schema
- **Visualization:** Tableau
- **Documentation:** GitHub (case-study format)

---

## 8. What Differentiates This Solution

Unlike typical BI dashboards, this project:

- Focuses on **revenue impact**, not just reporting
- Aligns data across **finance, sales, and customer success**
- Emphasizes **decision-making over visualization**
- Demonstrates a **full end-to-end analytics workflow**

---

## 9. Conclusion

Revenue leakage is often **invisible until it becomes material**.

This solution transforms fragmented data into a **cohesive, decision-ready system** that enables organizations to:

- Protect revenue
- Improve retention
- Act faster and with greater confidence
