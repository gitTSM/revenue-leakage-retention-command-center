# Business Case

## Background
A mid-sized B2B organization is experiencing stagnant revenue growth despite consistent customer acquisition. While top-line revenue appears stable, leadership has identified increasing volatility in renewals, payment cycles, and customer retention.

Preliminary analysis suggests that revenue leakage is occurring across multiple stages of the customer lifecycle, including renewals, billing, discounting, and customer engagement.

## Problem Statement
The organization lacks a unified, data-driven view of revenue performance and cannot accurately quantify or diagnose revenue leakage across customers, products, and operational processes.

As a result, leadership is unable to:
- identify the primary drivers of revenue loss
- prioritize high-risk customers and accounts
- take proactive action to improve retention and revenue realization

## Business Impact
The absence of clear visibility into revenue leakage leads to:

- missed revenue targets and reduced growth efficiency  
- inaccurate forecasting and planning  
- reactive (instead of proactive) customer retention strategies  
- inefficient allocation of sales and customer success resources  
- hidden financial losses due to discounting, churn, and delayed payments  

## Objective
Design and deliver an end-to-end analytics solution that:

- quantifies total revenue leakage across the organization  
- identifies key drivers of revenue loss across customer segments and operational processes  
- enables early detection of at-risk customers and revenue streams  
- supports data-driven decision-making for revenue recovery and retention strategies  

## Success Criteria
The solution will be considered successful if it enables stakeholders to:

- clearly measure revenue leakage and its components  
- identify top drivers of revenue loss by segment, product, and region  
- prioritize high-risk customers for intervention  
- monitor trends in retention, churn, and revenue realization over time  
- take actionable steps to improve revenue performance
