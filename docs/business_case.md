# Business Case

## Background
A mid-sized B2B organization is experiencing stagnant revenue growth despite stable customer acquisition. Leadership suspects that revenue leakage is occurring across the customer lifecycle.

## Problem Statement
The organization lacks a unified, data-driven view of revenue performance and cannot clearly identify where and why revenue is being lost.

## Business Impact
- Missed revenue targets
- Reduced forecasting accuracy
- Inefficient allocation of resources
- Increased customer churn risk

## Objective
Develop an end-to-end analytics solution to quantify revenue leakage, identify root causes, and enable proactive retention strategies.
